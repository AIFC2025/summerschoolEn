PROGRAM OVERVIEW


AI Action Summer School 2025, organized by the Institute of Natural Sciences, Shanghai Jiao Tong University, is a structured and interdisciplinary summer program focused on artificial intelligence. It is designed for senior undergraduate students, graduate students, and industry professionals.

The program offers a comprehensive learning experience—ranging from theoretical foundations and practical applications to innovation, entrepreneurship, and frameworks for AI governance. Through a well-designed curriculum, team-based projects, and visits to leading AI companies and research laboratories, participants will gain a deep understanding of core AI technologies and their applications in scientific research, industry, and social governance. Participants will also have the opportunity to engage closely with leading research institutions, industry experts, and entrepreneurial mentors —building valuable academic and professional connections.

Supported by the Association d'Intelligence Artificielle France-Chine (AIFC), the program also features cultural exchange and networking activities. For students interested in pursuing further studies in France, it offers informational resources to help them better understand French culture and prepare for future academic and career paths.

COURSE HIGHLIGHTS

AI Action Summer School 2025 aims to provide interdisciplinary education in artificial intelligence, covering theory, technology, industrial applications, and governance frameworks.

Sino-French Collaboration · Merging Academia and Practice

Professors and experts from top institutions including Shanghai Jiao Tong University, Université Paris Sciences et Lettres (PSL), and Université Paris-Saclay come together to deliver a dual-track curriculum that integrates academic rigor with hands-on practice.

Five Core Modules · Building an AI Knowledge Framework

The curriculum spans machine learning, cutting-edge AI applications, AI for Science, AI governance, and innovation & entrepreneurship—offering a panoramic view of key pathways in AI development.

On-site Visits · Exploring Real-World Applications

Participants will visit leading AI companies and research institutions to experience real-world technological applications and deployment. Meet top researchers and entrepreneurs, and gain access to internship and career opportunities.

Project Challenges · Unleashing Innovative Potential

Work in teams to tackle real-world problems under expert mentorship. Outstanding projects may lead to publications, patents, or startup ideas, with top performers eligible for recommendation letters from faculty mentors.

Dual Certification · Boosting Academic and Career Prospects

Participants who successfully complete the program will receive a Non-degree Certificate from Shanghai Jiao Tong University and a Certificate of Recognition from the AIFC—strengthening future graduate school and job applications.

Mentor Interaction · Expanding International Networks

Engage in in-depth discussions with Chinese and French mentors, explore pathways for international study and employment, and build a diverse, global professional network to support your future career.

8. ORGANIZER AND SUPPORTER

Organizer: Institute of Natural Sciences, Shanghai Jiao Tong University

The Institute of Natural Sciences at Shanghai Jiao Tong University was established in 2010. Its core research areas include scientific and engineering computation, materials science; quantum dynamics, quantum information, and quantum computing; AI algorithms and theory, AI for Science; theoretical and computational neuroscience, computational biology; soft matter physics and biophysical processes in biological systems; probability and mathematical statistics, biostatistics, bioinformatics; analysis of fluid mechanics equations; plasma physics, among others.

The Institute is a key participant in the Shanghai National Center for Applied Mathematics (SJTU Branch) and the Ministry of Education Key Laboratory of Scientific and Engineering Computation. It has established the SJTU-Gensci Digital Healthcare Innovation Lab, the Soft Matter Biophysics Laboratory, and has led the founding of the Shanghai Jiao Tong University Chongqing Artificial Intelligence Institute, fostering significant scientific and technological achievements that promote industrial restructuring.

In recent years, the Institute has made a series of breakthrough advancements in AI driven by advanced algorithms, while actively promoting the popularization of AI scientific knowledge. It has been designated as the Minhang District AI and Applied Mathematics Popular Science Education Base, and through organizing programs such as the AI For Science Summer School, provides students and researchers with opportunities to deeply understand the cutting-edge developments and applications of artificial intelligence.

Supporting Organization: Association d'Intelligence Artificielle France-Chine(AIFC)

AIFC is a non-profit organization officially registered in Paris in July 2024, in accordance with the French Law of Associations of 1901.

For this program, AIFC brings together faculty resources from several prestigious French universities, including Paris Dauphine University (Paris IX), École Nationale Supérieure d'Électronique et de Radioélectricité de Grenoble (ENSE3), and Université Paris-Saclay. The association has developed a high-level curriculum covering cutting-edge theory and practical applications, supporting the cultivation of AI talent and fostering international academic exchange.
